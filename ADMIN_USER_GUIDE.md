# Admin User Guide

## Sign In

Go to `/admin/login`, enter your email and password, then open Dashboard.

## Main Areas

- Dashboard: content counts, warnings and recent activity.
- Pages: edit titles, descriptions, URLs, status, SEO basics, page HTML snapshots and structured sections.
- Blog: edit post titles, excerpts, article body, slugs, status and SEO basics.
- Services, Tools, Certificates, FAQs and Testimonials: edit the structured records that feed website modules.
- Media: upload files, edit metadata, add alt text and mark decorative images.
- Navigation, Footer, SEO, Google Integrations, Forms and Settings: edit site-wide structured settings.
- Redirects: add or edit old-to-new URL mappings.
- Settings: change your password and edit site settings.

## Editing A Page

1. Open Pages.
2. Select Edit.
3. Change the title, description, status, path, slug, page body or SEO fields.
4. Keep “Create a permanent redirect” enabled when changing a public URL.
5. Save.

The public page design is controlled by the site components and HTML snapshots, so editors do not manage CSS, JavaScript, fonts, colors or breakpoints.
Advanced fields such as Page HTML and Structured sections are powerful; change copy, links and image references carefully.

## Uploading Media

Open Media, choose a file, add a clear internal title and alt text, then upload. If an image is decorative, mark it decorative instead of writing alt text.

## Passwords

Open Settings to change your password. Use at least 12 characters.

## Safe Editing Notes

- Do not paste tracking scripts into content fields.
- Do not upload executable files.
- Do not change published URLs unless a redirect is needed.
- Do not add fake testimonials, ratings or credentials.
